Dragon

A dragon is a legendary creature, typically scaled or fire-spewing.

  • Etymology: Latin draconem meaning "huge serpent"
  • Average wings span: 18 meters
  • Average life span: 3000 years
  • Favourite food: Virgins

Smaug, considered the last “great” dragon to exist in Middle-earth, is a fire drake (another term for fire-breathing dragon.) Smaug was attracted to the wealth gathered by the Dwarves of the Lonely Mountain during King Thror’s reign, hoarding the treasures to himself for nearly 200 hundred years.
